LIBRARY TECHNOLOGY FOCUSED ON STRATEGY AND INNOVATION

Let ByWater Help You Align Your Technology to Your Strategic Goals

  • Data Migration & Manipulation
  • Skilled manipulation of library data is the key to a successful migration. Because it is the most important representation of a library's collection, we work closely with each library to ensure there are no duplicate entries and to consistently use the highest quality records for complete accuracy, identifying and manipulating each record for the correct encoding and diacritical display. In addition, each of our new customers receives a test system during the migration process to practice with and to be trained on.
  • Expert Anytime Koha Support
  • Quality of support is the most important focus of our company. Our top rated 24/7 support package is completely comprehensive and ensures the best possible Koha experience for our customers. We have the lowest customer to support personnel ratio in the business
  • Comprehensive Koha Training
  • Our company is dedicated to the expert training and implementation of Koha. All ByWater Solutions training sessions are led by professional MLS librarians and accomplished Koha trainers. We provide multiple formats for different learning styles including on-site, hands-on, classroom style learning, on-line presentations and demonstrations, and internally produced training manuals.

Your Technology Should Be Innovative; ByWater Can Help

  • Custom Koha Installations
  • To ensure the security of your data, ByWater Solutions offers a complete hosted environment on ByWater equipment placed in the virtual networking cloud. This also delivers improved performance for both your staff and your users. Other functions include the maintenance of your Linux server, the management of databases with real-time backups, and the installation of updates and upgrades to both the Linux server and your Koha ILS software. We also have options for installing Koha on local servers at your library. With the correct remote access we could completely manage the installation at your library
  • Koha Hosting Services
  • ByWater Solutions performs a daily backup of all system data stored on the cloud. This information is saved both on-site. In addition daily backups, we create a mirrored image of the system data and code-base on our servers located at the ByWater main office in California (US based customers only). If you so choose, we can also set up an additional system backup onsite at your library's location and populate that daily for an additional fee.
  • Custom Koha Development
  • ByWater Solutions fully embraces the ideals and practices of open source software. All custom development sponsored by our customers and completed by our company will be publicly available at all times, even while still in progress, in the ByWater Git repository. After the code has been tested and released to our customer for production use, we then publish the code to the community either in the form of a patch (for smaller code changes) or a pull request from our Git Repo.
